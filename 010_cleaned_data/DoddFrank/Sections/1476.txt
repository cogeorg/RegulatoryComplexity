                          RELATING TO CERTAIN APPRAISAL FEES.    Section 4 of the Real Estate Settlement Procedures Act of 1974 &lt;&lt;NOTE: 12 USC 2603.&gt;&gt; is amended by adding at the end the following new subsection:    ``(c) The standard form described in subsection (a) may include, in the case of an appraisal coordinated by an appraisal management company (as such term is defined in section 1121(11) of the Financial Institutions Reform, Recovery, and Enforcement Act of 1989 (12 U.S.C. 3350(11))), a clear disclosure of--            ``(1) the fee paid directly to the appraiser by such         company; and            ``(2) the administration fee charged by such company.''.
