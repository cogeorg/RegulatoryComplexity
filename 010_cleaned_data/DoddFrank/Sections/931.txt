    (a) Study.--The Comptroller General of the United States shall conduct a study on the impact of authorizing a private right of action against any person who aids or abets another person in violation of the securities laws. To the extent feasible, this study shall include--            (1) a review of the role of secondary actors in companies         issuance of securities;            (2) the courts interpretation of the scope of liability for         secondary actors under Federal securities laws after January 14,         2008; and            (3) the types of lawsuits decided under the Private         Securities Litigation Act of 1995.    (b) Report.--Not later than 1 year after the date of enactment of this Act, the Comptroller General shall submit a report to Congress on the findings of the study required under subsection (a).[[Page 124 STAT. 1872]]  Subtitle C--Improvements to the Regulation of Credit Rating Agencies
