                          MORTGAGES.    (a) Prepayment Penalty Provisions.-- &lt;&lt;NOTE: Repeal.&gt;&gt; Section 129(c)(2) of the Truth in Lending Act (15 U.S.C. 1639(c)(2)) is hereby repealed.    (b) No Balloon Payments.--Section 129(e) of the Truth in Lending Act (15 U.S.C. 1639(e)) is amended to read as follows:    ``(e) No Balloon Payments.--No high-cost mortgage may contain a scheduled payment that is more than twice as large as the average of earlier scheduled payments. This subsection shall not apply when the payment schedule is adjusted to the seasonal or irregular income of the consumer.''.
