    Section 202(a) of the Investment Advisers Act of 1940 (15 U.S.C. 80b-2) is amended by adding at the end the following:            ``(29) The terms `commodity pool', `commodity pool         operator', `commodity trading advisor', `major swap         participant', `swap', `swap dealer', and `swap execution         facility' have the same meanings as in section 1a of the         Commodity Exchange Act (7 U.S.C. 1a).''.
