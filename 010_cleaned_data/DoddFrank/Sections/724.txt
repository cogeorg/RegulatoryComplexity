    (a) Clearing Requirement.--            (1) In general.--Section 2 of the Commodity Exchange Act (7         U.S.C. 2) is amended--                    (A) by striking subsections (d), (e), (g), and (h);                 and                    (B) by redesignating subsection (i) as subsection                 (g).
