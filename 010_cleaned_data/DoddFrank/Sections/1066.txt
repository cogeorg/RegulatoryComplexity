    (a) Incidental Transfers Authorized.--The Director of the Office of Management and Budget, in consultation with the Secretary, shall make such additional incidental transfers and dispositions of assets and liabilities held, used, arising from, available, or to be made available, in connection with the functions transferred by this title, as the Director may determine necessary to accomplish the purposes of this title.    (b) Sunset.--The authority provided in this section shall terminate 5 years after the date of enactment of this Act.
