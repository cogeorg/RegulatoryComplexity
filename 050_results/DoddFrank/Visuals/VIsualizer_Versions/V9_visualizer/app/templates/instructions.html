Purpose

Classify the logical parts of a sentence.

  • A-Antecedent: antecedent parts describe cases in which the law can be applied.
  • C-Consequent: consequent parts describe the provision of the law.
  • T1-Topic: topic part 1 is a subject related to the antecedent part.
  • T2-Topic: topic part 2 is a subject related to the consequent part.
  • T3-Topic: topic part 3 is a subject related to both, the antecedent and the consequent part.
  • EL-LeftEquivalent: left equivalent is the left part of a sentence with two equivalent statements.
  • ER-RightEquivalent: right equivalent is the right part of a sentence with two equivalent statements.

Examples

Example 1

A securities holding company that is required by a foreign regulator or provision of foreign law to be subject to comprehensive consolidated supervision may register with the Board of Governors under paragraph (2) to become a supervised securities holding company.
