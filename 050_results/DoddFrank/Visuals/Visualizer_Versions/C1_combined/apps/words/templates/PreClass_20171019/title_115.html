TITLE 370. Housing Act of 1948.
Section 502(c) of the Housing Act of 1948 (12 U.S.C. 1701c(c)) is amended
(1) in the matter preceding paragraph (1), by striking "and the Director of the Office of Thrift Supervision" and inserting ", the Comptroller of the Currency, and the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation"; and
(2) in paragraph (3), by striking "Board" and inserting "Agency".
