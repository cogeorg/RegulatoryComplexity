class = "ex1">TITLE 714. Abusive swaps.
class = "ex5">The Commodity Futures Trading Commission or the Securities and Exchange Commission, or both, individually may, by rule or order
class = "ex6">(1) collect information as may be necessary concerning the markets for any types of
class = "ex7">(A) swap (as defined in section 1a of the Commodity Exchange Act (7 U.S.C. 1a)); or
class = "ex7">(B) security-based swap (as defined in section 1a of the Commodity Exchange Act (7 U.S.C. 1a)); and
class = "ex6">(2) issue a report with respect to any types of swaps or security-based swaps that the Commodity Futures Trading Commission or the Securities and Exchange Commission determines to be detrimental to
class = "ex7">(A) the stability of a financial market; or
class = "ex7">(B) participants in a financial market.
