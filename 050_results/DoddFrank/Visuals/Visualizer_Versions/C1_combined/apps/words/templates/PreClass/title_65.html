class = "ex1">TITLE 301. Purposes.
class = "ex5">The purposes of this title are
class = "ex6">(1) to provide for the safe and sound operation of the banking system of the United States;
class = "ex6">(2) to preserve and protect the dual system of Federal and State-chartered depository institutions;
class = "ex6">(3) to ensure the fair and appropriate supervision of each depository institution, regardless of the size or type of charter of the depository institution; and
class = "ex6">(4) to streamline and rationalize the supervision of depository institutions and the holding companies of depository institutions.
