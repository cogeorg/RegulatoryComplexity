class = "ex1">TITLE 965. Compliance examiners.
class = "ex5">Section 4 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 (15 U.S.C. 78d) is amended by adding at the end the following:
class = "amended">
class = "ex5">(h) Examiners.
class = "ex6">(1) Division of Trading and Markets. The Division of Trading and Markets of the Commission, or any successor organizational unit, shall have a staff of examiners who shall
class = "ex7">(A) perform compliance inspections and examinations of entities under the jurisdiction of that Division; and
class = "ex7">(B) report to the Director of that Division.
class = "ex6">(2) Division of Investment Management. The Division of Investment Management of the Commission, or any successor organizational unit, shall have a staff of examiners who shall
class = "ex7">(A) perform compliance inspections and examinations of entities under the jurisdiction of that Division; and
class = "ex7">(B) report to the Director of that Division.
