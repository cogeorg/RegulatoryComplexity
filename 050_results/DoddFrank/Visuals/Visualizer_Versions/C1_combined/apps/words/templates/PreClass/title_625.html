class = "ex1">TITLE 1506. Study on core deposits and brokered deposits.
class = "ex5">(a) Study. The Corporation shall conduct a study to evaluate
class = "ex6">(1) the definition of core deposits for the purpose of calculating the insurance premiums of banks;
class = "ex6">(2) the potential impact on the Deposit Insurance Fund of revising the definitions of brokered deposits and core deposits to better distinguish between them;
class = "ex6">(3) an assessment of the differences between core deposits and brokered deposits and their role in the economy and banking sector of the United States;
class = "ex6">(4) the potential stimulative effect on local economies of redefining core deposits; and
class = "ex6">(5) the competitive parity between large institutions and community banks that could result from redefining core deposits.
class = "ex5">(b) Report to Congress. Not later than 1 year after the date of enactment of this Act, the Corporation shall submit to the Committee on Banking, Housing, and Urban Affairs of the Senate and the Committee on Financial Services of the House of Representatives a report on the results of the study under subsection (a) that includes legislative recommendations, if any, to address concerns arising in connection with the definitions of core deposits and brokered deposits.
