class = "ex1">TITLE 751. Energy and environmental markets advisory committee.
class = "ex5">Section 2(a) of the Commodity Exchange Act (7 U.S.C. 2(a)) (as amended by section 727) is amended by adding at the end the following:
class = "amended">
class = "ex6">(15) Energy and environmental markets advisory committee.
class = "ex7">(A) Establishment.
class = "ex8">(i) In general. An Energy and Environmental Markets Advisory Committee is hereby established.
class = "ex8">(ii) Membership. The Committee shall have 9 members.
class = "ex8">(iii) Activities. The Committees objectives and scope of activities shall be
class = "ex9">(I) to conduct public meetings;
class = "ex9">(II) to submit reports and recommendations to the Commission (including dissenting or minority views, if any); and
class = "ex9">(III) otherwise to serve as a vehicle for discussion and communication on matters of concern to exchanges, firms, end users, and regulators regarding energy and environmental markets and their regulation by the Commission.
class = "ex7">(B) Requirements.
class = "ex8">(i) In general. The Committee shall hold public meetings at such intervals as are necessary to carry out the functions of the Committee, but not less frequently than 2 times per year.
class = "ex8">(ii) Members. Members shall be appointed to 3-year terms, but may be removed for cause by vote of the Commission.
class = "ex7">(C) Appointment. The Commission shall appoint members with a wide diversity of opinion and who represent a broad spectrum of interests, including hedgers and consumers.
class = "ex7">(D) Reimbursement. Members shall be entitled to per diem and travel expense reimbursement by the Commission.
class = "ex7">(E) FACA. The Committee shall not be subject to the Federal Advisory Committee Act (5 U.S.C. App.).
