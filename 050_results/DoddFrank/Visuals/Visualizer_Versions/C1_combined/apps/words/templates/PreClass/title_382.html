class = "ex5">(a) Reports of commission.
class = "ex6">(1) Annual reports required. Not later than 6 months after the end of each fiscal year, the Commission shall publish and submit to Congress a report that
class = "ex7">(A) describes the responsibility of the management of the Commission for establishing and maintaining an adequate internal control structure and procedures for financial reporting; and
class = "ex7">(B) contains an assessment of the effectiveness of the internal control structure and procedures for financial reporting of the Commission during that fiscal year.
class = "ex6">(2) Attestation. The reports required under paragraph (1) shall be attested to by the Chairman and chief financial officer of the Commission.
class = "ex5">(b) Report by Comptroller General.
class = "ex6">(1) Report required. Not later than 6 months after the end of the first fiscal year after the date of enactment of this Act, the Comptroller General of the United States shall submit a report to Congress that assesses
class = "ex7">(A) the effectiveness of the internal control structure and procedures of the Commission for financial reporting; and
class = "ex7">(B) the assessment of the Commission under subsection (a)(1)(B).
class = "ex6">(2) Attestation. The Comptroller General shall attest to, and report on, the assessment made by the Commission under subsection (a).
class = "ex5">(c) Reimbursements for cost of reports.
class = "ex6">(1) Reimbursements required. The Commission shall reimburse the Government Accountability Office for the full cost of making the reports under subsection (b), as billed therefor by the Comptroller General.
class = "ex6">(2) Crediting and use of reimbursements. Such reimbursements shall
class = "ex7">(A) be credited to the appropriation account "Salaries and Expenses, Government Accountability Office" current when the payment is received; and
class = "ex7">(B) remain available until expended.
