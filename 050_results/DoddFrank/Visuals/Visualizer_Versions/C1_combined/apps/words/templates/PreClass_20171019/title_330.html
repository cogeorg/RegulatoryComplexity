TITLE 929L. Enhanced application of antifraud provisions.
The Securities Exchange Act of 1934 (15 U.S.C. 78a et seq.) is amended
(1) in section 9
(A) by striking "registered on a national securities exchange" each place that term appears and inserting "other than a government security";
(B) in subsection (b), by striking "by use of any facility of a national securities exchange,"; and
(C) in subsection (c), by inserting after "unlawful for any" the following: "broker, dealer, or";
(2) in section 10(a)(1), by striking "registered on a national securities exchange" and inserting "other than a government security"; and
(3) in section 15(c)(1)(A), by striking "otherwise than on a national securities exchange of which it is a member".
