class = "ex1">TITLE 744. Restitution remedies.
class = "ex5">Section 6c(d) of the Commodity Exchange Act (7 U.S.C. 13a1(d)) is amended by adding at the end the following:
class = "amended">
class = "ex6">(3) Equitable remedies. In any action brought under this section, the Commission may seek, and the court may impose, on a proper showing, on any person found in the action to have committed any violation, equitable remedies including
class = "ex7">(A) restitution to persons who have sustained losses proximately caused by such violation (in the amount of such losses); and
class = "ex7">(B) disgorgement of gains received in connection with such violation.
