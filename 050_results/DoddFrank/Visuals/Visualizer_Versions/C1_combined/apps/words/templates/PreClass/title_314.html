class = "ex1">TITLE 926. Disqualifying felons and other. "bad actors" from Regulation D offerings
class = "ex5">Not later than 1 year after the date of enactment of this Act, the Commission shall issue rules for the disqualification of offerings and sales of securities made under section 230.506 of title 17, Code of Federal Regulations, that
class = "ex6">(1) are substantially similar to the provisions of section 230.262 of title 17, Code of Federal Regulations, or any successor thereto; and
class = "ex6">(2) disqualify any offering or sale of securities by a person that
class = "ex7">(A) is subject to a final order of a State securities commission (or an agency or officer of a State performing like functions), a State authority that supervises or examines banks, savings associations, or credit unions, a State insurance commission (or an agency or officer of a State performing like functions), an appropriate Federal banking agency, or the National Credit Union Administration, that
class = "ex8">(i) bars the person from
class = "ex9">(I) association with an entity regulated by such commission, authority, agency, or officer;
class = "ex9">(II) engaging in the business of securities, insurance, or banking; or
class = "ex9">(III) engaging in savings association or credit union activities; or
class = "ex8">(ii) constitutes a final order based on a violation of any law or regulation that prohibits fraudulent, manipulative, or deceptive conduct within the 10-year period ending on the date of the filing of the offer or sale; or
class = "ex7">(B) has been convicted of any felony or misdemeanor in connection with the purchase or sale of any security or involving the making of any false filing with the Commission.
