TITLE 333. Enhanced access to information for deposit insurance purposes.
(a) Section 7(a)(2)(B) of the Federal Deposit Insurance Act is amended by striking "agreement" and inserting "consultation".
(b) Section 7(b)(1)(E) of the Federal Deposit Insurance Act is amended
(1) in clause (i), by striking "such as" and inserting "including"; and
(2) in clause (iii), by striking "Corporation" and inserting "Corporation, except as provided in section 7(a)(2)(B)".
