class = "ex1">TITLE 936. Qualification standards for credit rating analysts.
class = "ex5">Not later than 1 year after the date of enactment of this Act, the Commission shall issue rules that are reasonably designed to ensure that any person employed by a nationally recognized statistical rating organization to perform credit ratings
class = "ex6">(1) meets standards of training, experience, and competence necessary to produce accurate ratings for the categories of issuers whose securities the person rates; and
class = "ex6">(2) is tested for knowledge of the credit rating process.
