class = "ex1">TITLE 327B. Deputy Comptroller for the supervision and examination of Federal savings associations.
class = "ex5">The Comptroller of the Currency shall designate a Deputy Comptroller, who shall be responsible for the supervision and examination of Federal savings associations.
