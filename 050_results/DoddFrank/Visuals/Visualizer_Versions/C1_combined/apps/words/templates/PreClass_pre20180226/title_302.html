class = "ex1">TITLE 918. Study regarding mutual fund advertising.
class = "ex5">(a) In general. The Comptroller General of the United States shall conduct a study on mutual fund advertising to identify
class = "ex6">(1) existing and proposed regulatory requirements for open-end investment company advertisements;
class = "ex6">(2) current marketing practices for the sale of open-end investment company shares, including the use of past performance data, funds that have merged, and incubator funds;
class = "ex6">(3) the impact of such advertising on consumers; and
class = "ex6">(4) recommendations to improve investor protections in mutual fund advertising and additional information necessary to ensure that investors can make informed financial decisions when purchasing shares.
class = "ex5">(b) Report. Not later than 18 months after the date of enactment of this Act, the Comptroller General of the United States shall submit a report on the results of the study conducted under subsection (a) to
class = "ex6">(1) the Committee on Banking, Housing, and Urban Affairs of the United States Senate; and
class = "ex6">(2) the Committee on Financial Services of the House of Representatives.
