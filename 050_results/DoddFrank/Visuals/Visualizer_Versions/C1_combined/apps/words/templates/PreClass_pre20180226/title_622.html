class = "ex1">TITLE 1503. Reporting requirements regarding coal or other mine safety.
class = "ex5">(a) Reporting mine safety information. Each issuer that is required to file reports pursuant to section 13(a) or 15(d) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 (15 U.S.C. 78m, 78o) and that is an operator, or that has a subsidiary that is an operator, of a coal or other mine shall include, in each periodic report filed with the Commission under the securities laws on or after the date of enactment of this Act, the following information for the time period covered by such report:
class = "ex6">(1) For each coal or other mine of which the issuer or a subsidiary of the issuer is an operator
class = "ex7">(A) the total number of violations of mandatory health or safety standards that could significantly and substantially contribute to the cause and effect of a coal or other mine safety or health hazard under section 104 of the Federal Mine Safety and Health Act of 1977 (30 U.S.C. 814) for which the operator received a citation from the Mine Safety and Health Administration;
class = "ex7">(B) the total number of orders issued under section 104(b) of such Act (30 U.S.C. 814(b));
class = "ex7">(C) the total number of citations and orders for unwarrantable failure of the mine operator to comply with mandatory health or safety standards under section 104(d) of such Act (30 U.S.C. 814(d));
class = "ex7">(D) the total number of flagrant violations under section 110(b)(2) of such Act (30 U.S.C. 820(b)(2));
class = "ex7">(E) the total number of imminent danger orders issued under section 107(a) of such Act (30 U.S.C. 817(a));
class = "ex7">(F) the total dollar value of proposed assessments from the Mine Safety and Health Administration under such Act (30 U.S.C. 801 et seq.); and
class = "ex7">(G) the total number of mining-related fatalities.
class = "ex6">(2) A list of such coal or other mines, of which the issuer or a subsidiary of the issuer is an operator, that receive written notice from the Mine Safety and Health Administration of
class = "ex7">(A) a pattern of violations of mandatory health or safety standards that are of such nature as could have significantly and substantially contributed to the cause and effect of coal or other mine health or safety hazards under section 104(e) of such Act (30 U.S.C. 814(e)); or
class = "ex7">(B) the potential to have such a pattern.
class = "ex6">(3) Any pending legal action before the Federal Mine Safety and Health Review Commission involving such coal or other mine.
class = "ex5">(b) Reporting shutdowns and patterns of violations. Beginning on and after the date of enactment of this Act, each issuer that is an operator, or that has a subsidiary that is an operator, of a coal or other mine shall file a current report with the Commission on Form 8K (or any successor form) disclosing the following regarding each coal or other mine of which the issuer or subsidiary is an operator:
class = "ex6">(1) The receipt of an imminent danger order issued under section 107(a) of the Federal Mine Safety and Health Act of 1977 (30 U.S.C. 817(a)).
class = "ex6">(2) The receipt of written notice from the Mine Safety and Health Administration that the coal or other mine has
class = "ex7">(A) a pattern of violations of mandatory health or safety standards that are of such nature as could have significantly and substantially contributed to the cause and effect of coal or other mine health or safety hazards under section 104(e) of such Act (30 U.S.C. 814(e)); or
class = "ex7">(B) the potential to have such a pattern.
class = "ex5">(c) Rule of construction. Nothing in this section shall be construed to affect any obligation of a person to make a disclosure under any other applicable law in effect before, on, or after the date of enactment of this Act.
class = "ex5">(d) Commission Authority.
class = "ex6">(1) Enforcement. A violation by any person of this section, or any rule or regulation of the Commission issued under this section, shall be treated for all purposes in the same manner as a violation of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 (15 U.S.C. 78a et seq.) or the rules and regulations issued thereunder, consistent with the provisions of this section, and any such person shall be subject to the same penalties, and to the same extent, as for a violation of such Act or the rules or regulations issued thereunder.
class = "ex6">(2) Rules and regulations. The Commission is authorized to issue such rules or regulations as are necessary or appropriate for the protection of investors and to carry out the purposes of this section.
class = "ex5">(e) Definitions. In this section
class = "ex6">(1) the terms "issuer" and "securities laws" have the meaning given the terms in section 3 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 (15 U.S.C. 78c);
class = "ex6">(2) the term "coal or other mine" means a coal or other mine, as defined in section 3 of the Federal Mine Safety and Health Act of 1977 (30 U.S.C. 802), that is subject to the provisions of such Act (30 U.S.C. 801 et seq.); and
class = "ex6">(3) the term "operator" has the meaning given the term in section 3 of the Federal Mine Safety and Health Act of 1977 (30 U.S.C. 802).
class = "ex5">(f) Effective date. This section shall take effect on the day that is 30 days after the date of enactment of this Act.
