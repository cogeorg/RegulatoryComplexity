TITLE 356. Bank Protection Act of 1968.
The Bank Protection Act of 1968 (12 U.S.C. 1881 et seq.) is amended
(1) in section 2 (12 U.S.C. 1881), by striking "the term" and all that follows through the end of the section and inserting "the term"; "Federal supervisory agency" means the appropriate Federal banking agency, as defined in section 3(q) of the Federal Deposit Insurance Act (12 U.S.C. 1813(q)).
(2) in section 3 (12 U.S.C. 1882), by striking "and loan" each place that term appears; and
(3) in section 5 (12 U.S.C. 1884), by striking "and loan".
