Purpose

Classify entities and definitions.

  • Regulator: Terms of class Regulator are regulating entities.
  • Regulated: Terms of class Regulated are regulated entities.
  • Definition: Terms of class Definition are terms that are defined in the act. This may not only be explicit definitions (The term "Council" means [...]) but also implicit definitions (The voting members, [...] shall be [...]).
  • Legal Reference: Terms of class Legal Reference are references to other legal texts.

How it works

Classification:

  • Select a term.
  • Click on the appropriate classification on the right-hand side.
  • The part is colored accordingly.
  • Do not hesitate to override an already existing classification (sometimes, a regulating or regulated entity is inside a legal reference.)

Updating:

  • If you want to have all instances of a classified term colored, click on the Button "Update".
  • All instances are colored and the classification is saved.

Corrections:

  • There are two possible ways:
    1. Select the term and click on "Remove" on the bottom-right.
    2. Right click on any instance of the term.
  • All instances are deleted if you click "Update" afterwards.
  • Now, you may classify it again.
