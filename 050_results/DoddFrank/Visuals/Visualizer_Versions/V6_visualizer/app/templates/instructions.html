Purpose

    Classify the words depending on their function and gramatical behaviour.
  • Grammatical Operators: Natural language words which have very little meaning, such as "the", "a", "an", "are" and similar words.
  • Logical Operators: Words which work as operators in text, it includes conditional words such as "if" , "or", "when", comparison words such as "equivalent", "same", "similar" and reference words such as "with respect to", "for purposes of".
  • Legal Operators: Common words used in the regulatory texts which work as operands. It includes "rule", "title", "inserting"

Features

  • Highlighting of selected text: Select and pick the color for each word(s).
  • Update and find the list of words: Store the list of words highlighted during your session and find them in the document.
  • Remove words: Select and remove words already highlighted.
  • Change title: Select different titles.
  • Check the list of words classified during your session.

How it works?

  • Select the word.
  • Right click.
  • Choose the type of word or the task from the menu
